About Us

Founded in 2022, Bulk Green is dedicated to revolutionizing the Retail industry with sustainable bulk dispensers. Our mission is to reduce plastic waste and promote eco-friendly shopping habits whilst enhancing the retailers logistics and supply chain efficiency. By focusing on innovation and sustainability, we aim to make a significant impact on the environment and change the way consumers shop.
